About Me

Welcome to my Data Science portfolio website! I'm Kuro Peter Doumu, a passionate data scientist specializing in transforming raw data into actionable insights. With expertise in MS Excel, Python, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau, I excel at uncovering patterns and delivering data-driven solutions.

Throughout my career, I have successfully utilized my skills in statistical analysis, data visualization, and predictive modeling to optimize processes and enhance business outcomes. With experience across diverse industries like marketing, finance, and healthcare, I am an active learner and adapt quickly in order to provide valuable insights.
I really enjoy data storytelling. Using tools like Power BI and Tableau, I have created compelling dashboards and reports that effectively communicate complex information in a concise and understandable manner.

In addition to my technical expertise, I love travelling and enjoy immersing myself in different cultures and traditions. When I'm not analyzing data, you can find me engrossed in a good book or cheering for my favorite sports teams/players, including football, basketball, and tennis.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I invite you to explore my projects, showcasing my ability to analyze complex datasets, extract meaningful insights, and present them visually. I look forward to connecting with you and discussing how I can contribute to your organization's success through data informed decisions.
